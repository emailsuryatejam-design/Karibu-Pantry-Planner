## Schema Reference

- `name` — recipe name
- `category` — Appetizer | Soup | Main Course | Dessert
- `meal_type` — Lunch | Dinner | Both
- `tags` — vegetarian, vegan, fish, chicken, beef, lamb, pork, dessert
- `serves` — number of pax
- `description` — from menu card
- `ingredients` — item, unit, quantity
- `method` — cooking notes (where available)
- `plating` — serving notes (where available)
- `schedule` — days featured on weekly menu
